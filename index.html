Privacy Policy — Maze Runner

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This policy describes what data Maze Runner (“the App”) collects and how it is used. The App is published by Jeremie Agee. Contact: jeremieagee@gmail.com.

Data the App Collects

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App shows ads via Google AdMob (interstitial and rewarded video ads). AdMob collects the advertising identifier and other device/usage data to serve and measure ads. This data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps. We do not receive or store this data ourselves.

In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers a one-time “Remove Ads” purchase processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect, see, or store your payment information — that is handled by Google.

Leaderboard (Supabase)

If you submit a score to the online leaderboard, the App sends:

  • The display name you typed in for the leaderboard (defaults to a generic “Player” if you don't set one)
  • Your score, floor reached, run time, and death message
  • A random identifier generated once for your device installation (not tied to your name, account, or any other personal information)

This data is stored on our backend (Supabase) so scores can be shown to other players. The random device identifier is never shown to other players or returned by any leaderboard listing — it exists solely so you can request deletion of your own entries (see below). Leaderboard participation is optional; the App is fully playable without ever submitting a score.

Locally Stored Data

Your character customization, coin balance, purchase status, and leaderboard display name are stored only on your device (Android SharedPreferences) and are not transmitted anywhere except as described above.

Your Choices

  • Leaderboard name: set or change it anytime from the Leaderboard screen (“Edit Name”).
  • Delete your leaderboard data: from the Leaderboard screen, tap “Delete My Scores” to permanently delete every score you've submitted from that device. This cannot be undone.
  • Ads: purchase “Remove Ads” from the title screen to stop ad requests (this does not affect leaderboard data).

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
